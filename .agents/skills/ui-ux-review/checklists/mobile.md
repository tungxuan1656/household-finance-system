# Mobile UI Checklist

Check:
- Is the primary task visible without excessive scrolling?
- Are touch targets at least 44px tall?
- Is horizontal scrolling avoided?
- Are desktop tables converted into mobile cards?
- Are filters moved into a Sheet or Drawer?
- Is bottom navigation clear?
- Does the FAB overlap content or bottom navigation?
- Are sticky elements used sparingly?
- Are long labels shortened or wrapped gracefully?
- Is the page usable with one hand?
- Is there enough bottom padding?
- Are modals replaced with sheets when appropriate?
