# Finance App Checklist

Check:
- Are amounts clearly labeled?
- Is it clear whether a value is income, expense, balance, budget, or remaining amount?
- Is currency formatting consistent?
- Are date ranges human-readable?
- Are category colors consistent?
- Are income and expense visually distinguishable?
- Are percentages not misleading? Use `<1%` instead of `0%` when appropriate.
- Are empty states accurate to the actual data?
- Are duplicated CTAs avoided?
- Are transaction rows or cards scannable?
- Are destructive financial actions protected by confirmation?
