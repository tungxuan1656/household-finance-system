# Dashboard Checklist

Check:
- Does the dashboard answer "what changed?" or "what matters now?"
- Are KPI cards clearly labeled?
- Are date ranges clear?
- Are charts secondary to the most important numbers?
- Are chart legends readable?
- Are percentages rounded responsibly?
- Are recent activities easy to scan?
- Are empty states accurate?
- Are actions clearly separated from analytics?
- Is the dashboard too tall on mobile?
